⚡ Feature Overview

What JobsNext can do

Here are the main things in JobsNext. Tap a card to learn more.

🔍
Find jobs easily
Look at many jobs. Use filters like place, pay, and job type. Jone AI shows if a job fits you.
🤖
AI
AI Match Score
Jone AI reads your profile and the job. It shows how well the job fits you.
⚡
Apply fast
Use your saved profile to apply fast. Add your resume. Add a cover letter if you want. Then tap submit.
🔖
Save and check jobs
Save jobs you like. Find them later in the Saved tab. Apply from there.
📊
Check your applications

📝
Skill tests

📚
Career help

🏢

Employer home

Top companies

❓ FAQ

Questions and answers

Need more help? Open Help in the app.

🔍
Do I need an account to browse jobs? ▾
No. You can look at jobs without an account. Sign in only when you want to save a job or apply.
How does the AI match score work? ▾
Jone AI reads your profile and the job. It gives you a score. A full profile helps Jone AI give a better score.
Can I apply to multiple jobs at once? ▾
Yes. You can apply to many jobs. Save jobs first. Then open Saved and apply to them one by one.
How do I reset my password? ▾
On the login screen, tap Forgot password?. Type your email. Tap Send. Check your email. If you do not see it, check spam.
What is App Lock and how do I enable it? ▾
App Lock keeps the app safe. It can use Face ID, finger scan, or PIN. Go to Profile → Settings → App Lock and turn it on.
How do push notifications work? ▾
JobsNext can send phone alerts for new jobs, application updates, tests, and messages. Change alerts in Settings → Notifications.
How do employers post a job? ▾
Sign in as an employer. Tap the + button. Add the job details. Check it. Tap Publish. One job post uses job credits.
What are assessments? ▾
Assessments are skill tests. Employers make them. Jobseekers do them in the app. Employers see the score and answers.
Can I switch between Jobseeker and Employer modes? ▾
No. Jobseeker and Employer are different account types. Use a different email for each account.
How do I delete my account? ▾
Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Confirm with the email code. This removes your profile, saved jobs, and applications. Read the Account Deletion Policy, Privacy Policy, and Terms & Conditions.

💡 Tips & Best Practices

Helpful tips

Simple tips to help you use JobsNext well.

✅ Do
Finish your profile first
Fill in your profile before you apply. A full profile helps Jone AI find better jobs for you.
✅ Do
Use voice search on Browse
Tap the microphone on Browse. Say the job you want. This can be faster than typing.
✅ Do
Save first, apply later
Save jobs you like. Later, open Saved and apply to the best jobs first.
✅ Do
Turn on phone alerts
Turn on alerts. You can see new jobs and updates quickly.
⚠️ Avoid
Do not apply with an empty profile
If your profile is empty, employers see very little about you. Fill it in first.
⚠️ Avoid
Do not miss test dates
If you see "Assessment Due", do the test before the date. Set a reminder.
⚠️ Avoid
Make each cover letter fit the job
Use Jone AI to start a letter. Then edit it so it talks about this job and this company.
🔄 Plan
Easy daily plan
Morning: check alerts. Look for jobs. Save good jobs. Evening: apply to the best saved jobs. Check Applied.
🔄 Plan
Employers: send tests early
Send tests before interviews. This helps you choose the right people faster.
🔄 Plan
Keep your resume updated
Upload a new resume when you finish a new job, course, or project.
